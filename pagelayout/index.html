LCD Soundsystem:
The Last Show

About | Discography

In April 2011 LCD Soundsystem performed their last show at Madison Square Gardens in New York City. Surrounded by thousands of adoring fans, James Murphy and the gang belted out hit after hit during a three hour set of the band's full career history.

One of the highlights of the evening was when Win Butler and Regine Chassagne of Arcade Fire fame joined the band on stage for a rendition of North American Scum.

And then this happened.

People really did cry.
